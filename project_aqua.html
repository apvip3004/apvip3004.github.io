Fish tank and GUI application

Used technology:
- C\C++
- Qt
- ESP32

Equipment:
- Fish tank 120l

Up to this point, only the application and the fish tank has been completed.

Description of application:
The app provides automatic control of the aquarium. It enables control over:
- light
- 5x pumps
- temperature measurement
- PH measurement
- CO2 control
The aquarium has 120 litres. It is a freshwater planted aquarium including fishes.

Chemical parameters:
Temp 25
PH 7.5
GH 10
KH 7
NO3 10
PO4 0.5
K 15
