Application of eye tracking technology in electronic devices control.

Master Thesis. Designing and creating a control system for selected electronic devices through a dedicated application. The control is done by moving the eyeballs. The application was implemented in C ++ in the Qt environment. The target equipment used by the designed system is a laptop with a webcam. The camera transmits the image, which is processed into the appropriate coordinates. The application assumes the control of mini programs such as numeric keyboard, application supporting the life activities of the disabled, RGB diode control.
