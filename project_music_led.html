Studio Music Led

A device is used to light 3 zones in any RGB LED colour. The device has a built-in digital microphone that listens to sounds, then algorithms process the sound to the level of the selected animation.

Used technology:
- C, (Arduino, Bluetooth)
- AppInventor

Equipment:
- 3 RGB LED strips are addressable
- digital microphone
- controller with BT module
- Android application to control LEDs

Description of application:
The application can provide choosing the right training for the user.
Parameters of training:
- name
- the colour of the pointing light
- the colour of blocking light
- time of training
- time of lightning pointing colour
- time of lightning blocking colour
- the probability of blocking colour
- time of back to the start position
- active buttons

The application allows you to control 3 zones of RGB LED strips. A single zone can be set to:
1. Disabled
2. Constant lighting
3. Animations reacting to music (number of animations: 12)
4. Animations (number of animations 8)

You can also choose the colour and brightness of a single zone.
