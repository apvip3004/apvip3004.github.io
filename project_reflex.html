Reflex Machine

Device for measure and train time of reaction for sportsman.

Equipment:
- 14 mechanical buttons with RGB lights
- display 10'
- driver
- dedicated application to control buttons and enabling to compose the appriopriate training
- frame

Used technology:
- KiCad (Project of pcb, driver of buttons and rgb leds)
- RPi, Yocto
- Qt5 + QML, (Application)
- STM32 (driver for buttons)

Description of application:
The application can provide to choosing the right training for the user.
Parameters of traning:
- name
- color of pointing light
- color of blocking light
- time of training
- time of lightining pointing color
- time of lightining blocking color
- propability of blocking color
- time of back to the start position
- active buttons

The composed training can be saved and later can be load. The application allows you to create your own profile. After the traing is over , if any profile is logged in, statistics will be sent to the previously saved training. The application notifies the user if he wants to delete a training/profile or wants to save an already existing training with other parameters.
